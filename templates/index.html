About Homiefy

Homiefy is an all in one portal application that provides its users with a variety of at-home services to facilitate day to day living. These services include appliance repair, pest control, plumbing, electrical repair, and home cleaning to name a few. Users can browse, schedule, and manage appointments easily with a click of a button.

Homiefy provides valuable information about services including type of service, location, available hours, etc. parsed from online websites and Yelp API. Information regarding service ratings are also included and will be provided by users of our application.

Functionalities include browsing available services, updating statuses of services, allowing business partners to list additional services, deleting no longer existing services, as well as the utilization of a recommendation system.
